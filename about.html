About OPTCG Market

OPTCG Market was created by One Piece TCG collectors and players who were tired of paying 13% fees on every sale. We built the marketplace we wished existed — one where sellers keep 100% of the sale price and buyers get better deals because of it.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted, lowest-cost marketplace for One Piece TCG cards. We believe trading card communities work best when the platform doesn't take a cut of every transaction. That's why OPTCG Market charges 0% seller fees — and always will.

What We Offer

Zero fees. No listing fees, no final value fees, no payment processing markups. Sell a $100 card and keep $100.

Trust built in. Our vouch and ranking system means every completed trade builds your reputation. No anonymous sellers, no mystery buyers.

Middleman escrow. For high-value transactions, our trusted moderators hold payment until the buyer confirms delivery. It's like PayPal buyer protection, but free and built into Discord.

One community. Instead of joining 20 Facebook groups, OPTCG Market is one centralized hub for buying, selling, discussing the meta, showcasing pulls, and getting price checks.

Who Runs This?

OPTCG Market is run by a small team of dedicated One Piece TCG players and collectors. We're active in the community every day — buying, selling, and trading cards just like everyone else. We built this because we needed it ourselves.

Contact

The best way to reach us is through our Discord server. Open a ticket or message a moderator — we typically respond within a few hours.
